ABOUT ME

My philosophy in web design boils down to two principles: simplicity and versatility.

To do that, I prioritize the user experience. I develop lightweight websites for fast loading speed, and minimal but intentional design to encourage user engagement with your brand.

If you are looking for a web developer that understands your pain points, and develops a website that is simple for you to use but powerful enough to get you result, you can send me an email below for a consultation.

LITTLE ABOUT ME

My interest in new technologies started from a young age. However, I was persuaded by my parents to pursue a healthcare career. I earned my BS degree in Biological Science and started working, but I was unfulfilled.

After working for awhile, I realized that beside earning money, I need to enjoy my job too. I finally bit the bullet and pushed forward to earn my certification in Web Developing.

It’s amazing what can happen when you’re passionate about your work. Since then I have dive head first and deep into development my talents as a Web Developer.
